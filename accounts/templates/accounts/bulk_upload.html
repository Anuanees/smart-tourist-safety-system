CSV Format Reference
Danger Zones CSV Format:
name,latitude,longitude,severity,radius,description
Downtown Crime Zone,10.8505,76.2711,High,500,High crime area
Flood Prone Area,10.8600,76.2800,Medium,1000,Seasonal flooding
Validation Rules:
  • severity must be: Low, Medium, or High
  • latitude and longitude must be valid decimal numbers
  • radius must be a positive integer (in meters)
  • Empty rows will be skipped
  • Duplicates (same name + coordinates) will be skipped
Places CSV Format:
name,type,latitude,longitude,description
Central Hospital,Hospital,10.8505,76.2711,24/7 emergency services
City Police Station,Police Station,10.8600,76.2800,Main police headquarters
Validation Rules:
  • type must be: Police Station, Hospital, or Help Center
  • latitude and longitude must be valid decimal numbers
  • name cannot be empty
  • Empty rows will be skipped
  • Duplicates (same name + coordinates) will be skipped
